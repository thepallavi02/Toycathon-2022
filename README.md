# Toycathon 2021-22 (Physical-Edition)

Under the ‘AatmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan’ initiated by our Hon’ble Prime Minister, Shri. Narendra Modi, Toycathon-2021 was conceived to challenge India’s innovative minds to conceptualize novel Toy and Games based on Bharatiya civilization, history, culture, mythology and ethos.

## Insights of our Game:
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/89097874/179464461-d2fc82ae-0214-4cfb-ac72-b655f0ce830b.png)
<li>Name : HAMARA संविधान </li>
<br>
<b>Brief Description :</b>
<p><li>Hamara Samvidhan, board game comes with a comic book based on the constitution of India reflecting indian culture and ethos question cards, tokens along with a timer of 30 seconds and a pair of needles to spin and obtain the numbers to move.</li>
<li>To play, 2 to 4 players begin by placing their respective tokens in their houses.</li>
<li>Players take turns in a clockwise order.</li>
<li>Spinning needle, if highest value obtained, game begins.</li> 
<li>After each throw, the player decides which token to move and answer's the Q-card/taken card according to the position.</li>
<li>When a token has circumnavigated the board, it proceeds up to the winning house.</li>
<li>The first person to move all 2 tokens into the winning house wins.</li>
While progressing through the game, players discover a wealth of valuable information regarding India's constitutions.</p>
<li>Organized by Ministry of Education’s Innovation Cell with support from All India Council for Technical Education, Ministry of Women and Child Development, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Ministry of MSME, Ministry of Textiles and Ministry of Information and Broadcasting.</li>
